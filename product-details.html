Description

dermaOXY A-Link Defence Booster helps strengthen the skin's natural defense and supports regeneration. It reduces fine lines, wrinkles, and pigmentation while stimulating collagen production, leaving the skin silky smooth and nourished.

A-Link Defence Booster both prevents and repairs, among other things, sun damage and pigmentation changes. Its high content of vitamin A helps increase the skin's elasticity and softness. In addition, it has a beneficial effect on acne and other inflammatory conditions.

A-Link Defence Booster has the regenerating effect of vitamin A and can be used as both a day and night cream.

A-Link Defence Booster is recommended for daily use and works well in combination with Facecleanser Deep Clean and Moisturizing Gel.
