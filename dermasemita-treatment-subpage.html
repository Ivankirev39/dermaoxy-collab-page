How does LED light therapy work?

The skin has the ability to absorb light and convert it into cellular energy. Light energy stimulates the mitochondria of the cells and increases ATP production.

ATP (adenosine triphosphate) contains chemical energy and supplies energy to almost all energy-demanding processes in the body, such as collagen production, nutrient delivery, and blood circulation.

The difference between standard LED light and dermaSEMITA LED light therapy is that the skin is exposed to light at specific wavelengths, with a specific intensity and frequency, optimized to stimulate the skin’s cells in the most effective way.

dermaSEMITA delivers light in carefully selected wavelengths, each working at different depths of the skin and influencing different cells and biological processes.

dermaSEMITA Treatment Options

A dermaSEMITA treatment can be performed using different light wavelengths, which affect the depth of the treatment and how the skin’s cells are stimulated.

Red light – Collagen stimulation

Anti-aging, reduces redness, stimulates collagen production, smooths the skin.

Yellow light – Photo rejuvenation

Photo rejuvenation, strengthens lymphatic and blood circulation.

Blue light – Acne treatment

Treats acne, anti-inflammatory effect, can relieve itching.

Treatment Combinations

The different light therapies can be combined to treat multiple skin depths simultaneously.

Red light + Blue light

Yellow light + Red light

Blue light + Yellow light

Blue light + Yellow light
