About DermaOxy

dermaOXY is a brand from MedicTinedic ApS and consists of Danish-developed and produced skincare products that restore the skin's health and counteract wrinkles and dry skin using natural active ingredients. DermaOXY's treatments and care products provide moisture to the skin and scalp with active hyaluronic acid, a high content of antioxidants, and an active supply of vitamins that stimulate the skin's own cell and collagen production.

Future Skin Care

dermaOxy has great respect for the skin's natural structure and aim to demonstrate through our products that anti-aging can be achieved without cosmetic procedures. That is why they use oxygen—pure oxygen—in our treatments, helping to slow the skin's aging process and stimulate the production of elastin and collagen.

DermaOXY is part of the future of skincare, and you can see a difference after just one treatment.
